FAQs

Can I Join If I've Never Played Organized Basketball? +

Yes. Our D5 Rookie division is designed for beginners and anyone new to organized basketball. We welcome all skill levels — just show up ready to learn and compete.

Is There A 2KSL Location Near Me? +

We're currently operating in 10+ cities across the Greater Toronto Area and expanding. Check our locations page for the full list and upcoming cities.

Do I Get A Custom Jersey? +

Yes. Every 2KSL athlete receives a custom jersey with their name and number. Your jersey is yours to keep after the season.

What Are The 5 Skill Levels? +

D5 Rookie (beginner), D4 Rec (developing), D3 Inter (intermediate), D2 Comp (competitive), D1 Elite (top-tier). Every player can find their level and grow from there.

How Long Is A Season? +

Each 2KSL season runs for 10 weeks, with regular-season games followed by playoffs and a championship final. New seasons run throughout the year.

What's The Cost To Register? +

Pricing varies by city and division. Visit your local location page for exact pricing. Your season cost covers your custom jersey, 10 weeks of play, pro media, and championship celebrations.

What's The Refund Policy? +

Refund requests submitted before the season start date are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please review our full Refund Policy for details.

What's Included In My Season Registration? +

Your registration includes a custom jersey, 10 weeks of scheduled games, pro-level media and highlights, digital stats and leaderboards, and championship celebrations for winning teams.
